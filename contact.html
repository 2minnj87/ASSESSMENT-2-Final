These Terms and Conditions apply to the Solent Pizza's Quality Guarantee (“Quality Guarantee”).

The Quality Guarantee excludes any issues with a pizza related to the temperature of the product and/or the delivery time. The offer does not apply to sides, drinks or desserts.

To claim the Quality Guarantee:

No more than 2 slices of the pizza may have been eaten and the rest must be unaffected. Your pizza must be exchanged for the new one and you must show proof of purchase.

For delivery orders, you must use the Solent Pizza’s Quality Guarantee App or call the store where you originally placed the order within 30 minutes of the time of delivery subject to local store opening hours.

For collection orders, the Quality Guarantee must be claimed in-store at the time of collection.
